About Me

I am a web developer based in Minneapolis-St. Paul and graduate of the Full-Stack Web Development Program @ Thinkful. I absolutely love programming because I thrive in an environment where I can be both analytical: solving problems & creative: building useful applications. My current gig has me as Lead Developer with an emphasis on Angular, and it couldn't be more fun!

Seeing the hard work come to life on the page is extremely rewarding to me.

I feel my unique life experiences have shaped a creative outlook, hard work ethic, and a get it done attitude that employers will be excited about.

Prior to web development, and as an educator, I was always an advocate of life-long learning - I carry this into my day to day rituals and routines. Web development is more than just a job - It's a paid hobby!

Beyond web development, you can find me performing on trombone in various groups throughout the Minneapolis-St. Paul metro area, exploring nature, grilling meat, enjoying a local craft brew, or watching reruns of Star Trek: TNG.
