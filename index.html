About Me

I am a web developer based in Minneapolis-St. Paul and graduate of the Full-Stack Web Development Program @ Thinkful. I absolutely love programming because I thrive in an environment where I can be both analytical [solving problems] and creative {creating useful applications}.

Seeing this hard work come to life on the page is extremely rewarding to me.

I have a background in K-12 Music Education and worked as an inner-city band director before "retiring" from teaching to pursue web development full time. I feel my unique life experiences have shaped a creative outlook, hard work ethic, and a get it done attitude that employers will be excited about.

As an educator, I was always an advocate of life-long learning and I carry that into my day to day rituals and routines. I'm thrilled to now continue a life-long passion of mine full time!

Beyond web development, you can find me performing on trombone in various groups throughout the Minneapolis-St. Paul metro area, exploring nature, grilling meat, enjoying a local craft brew, or watching reruns of Star Trek: TNG.
